Boudoir Session

Stan and Jane are Wedding and Portrait Photographers located in North East England. They primarily photograph weddings in Co. Durham, North Yorkshire, Newcastle and Northumberland, but love to also venture further a field in the United Kingdom and beyond. Their work is regularly featured in Bridal Magazines and Wedding Blogs. They have a spacious Portrait Studio based in Darlington, Co. Durham and are happy to offer both Studio Portrait Shoots or Location Photography Shoots in some of the stunning countryside and coastal locations in the North East area.
