Testimonials

what people is saying about me ;)

Gergana Daskalova

I am a PhD student interested in biodiversity change, agroecology, landscape ecology, conservation policy and the potential of abandoned agricultural land for biodiversity conservation. My research project aims to quantify the effects of land use change on global and local patterns of species richness, abundance and composition. I am absolutely fascinated by the power of good education in helping people reach and exceed their potential and dream big – my own dream is to become a lecturer and researcher at least as inspirational and empowering as the mentors that I have been fortunate to have so far. For more information, check out my personal website and Twitter account @gndaskalova. I am part of the NERC E3 Doctoral Training Programme at the University of Edinburgh and I'm also a Carnegie scholar.
